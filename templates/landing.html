GraphCast Predictor

Next-Generation Global AI Weather Modeling

The Problem

Traditional Numerical Weather Prediction (NWP) models rely on extremely complex, computationally heavy physics equations. Forecasting extreme weather globally can take hours on massive supercomputer clusters, leading to critical delays in warning systems during rapidly evolving phenomena like cyclones and extreme precipitation events.

The AI Solution

We leverage GraphCast, a state-of-the-art AI built by Google DeepMind. By utilizing Graph Neural Networks (GNNs) on an icosahedral mesh of the Earth, the model bypasses classical physics calculations and natively infers atmospheric states directly from historical ERA5 climate data.

The result? Highly accurate, 10-day global forecasts generated in a mere fraction of the time, allowing for unprecedented insight into incoming severe weather anywhere on the globe.
